SnippetBoard is developed by buildwith.sreehari@gmail.com (“we”). We collect limited analytics and diagnostics only to improve reliability and user experience. We do not run our own backend, we do not show personalized ads, and we do not sell your data.

Details

Analytics and crash reporting are provided by trusted partners (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics or similar). Data is used in aggregate to understand app reliability and usage patterns. We do not combine this data with information that directly identifies you.

Data storage & retention

  • We do not operate our own servers for your app data.
  • Your app content remains on your device. (No iCloud sync in this version.)
  • Analytics/diagnostics are stored by our providers for limited periods under their default retention policies.

Tracking

We do not use data for cross‑app tracking or personalized advertising. We do not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission.

Children’s privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • You can delete the app to remove local data stored on your device.
  • If we add an in‑app analytics toggle in a future update, it will appear in Settings.

Contact

If you have any Questions or concerns regarding the Privacy Policy or the app, please feel free to contact us at buildwith.sreehari@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if we later add iCloud sync or in‑app purchases). We will update the date above.
